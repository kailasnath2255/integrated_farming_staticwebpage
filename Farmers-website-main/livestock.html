Livestock Information

Cattle

  • Animal Type: Cattle
  • Breed: Holstein
  • Age: 3 years
  • Health Records: Up-to-date vaccinations, no history of disease
  • Feed Requirements: 30 lbs of hay and 10 lbs of grain per day
  • Housing Conditions: Clean, spacious barn with adequate ventilation
  • Breeding Information: Artificial insemination used, calving interval of 12 months
  • Yield: 25 liters of milk per day
  • Market Value: ₹40,000 per cow
  • Veterinary Care: Monthly check-ups, access to emergency care

Goat

  • Animal Type: Goat
  • Breed: Boer
  • Age: 2 years
  • Health Records: Regular deworming, vaccinated for common diseases
  • Feed Requirements: 5 lbs of hay and 2 lbs of grain per day
  • Housing Conditions: Dry, well-ventilated shelter with space for grazing
  • Breeding Information: Natural breeding, kidding interval of 8 months
  • Yield: 1 gallon of milk per day, 50 lbs of meat per animal
  • Market Value: ₹10,000 per goat
  • Veterinary Care: Quarterly check-ups, access to emergency care

Pig

  • Animal Type: Pig
  • Breed: Yorkshire
  • Age: 1.5 years
  • Health Records: Vaccinated for swine flu, regular deworming
  • Feed Requirements: 6 lbs of feed per day
  • Housing Conditions: Clean pen with adequate space and shelter
  • Breeding Information: Artificial insemination or natural breeding, farrowing interval of 6 months
  • Yield: 200 lbs of meat per pig
  • Market Value: ₹15,000 per pig
  • Veterinary Care: Bi-monthly check-ups, access to emergency care
