Crop Information

  • (1)Crop Name: Corn
  • Variety: Sweet Corn
  • Planting Season: Spring
  • Harvesting Time: Late Summer
  • Soil Requirements: Well-drained loamy soil
  • Watering Needs: Moderate, every 5-7 days
  • Pesticide Use: Apply insecticides to control pests like corn borer
  • Yield Per Acre: 150 corns
  • Market Price: ₹50
  • Nutritional Information: High in carbohydrates, provides fiber and essential vitamins
  • (2)Crop Name: Tomato
  • Variety: Roma
  • Planting Season: Spring
  • Harvesting Time: Summer
  • Soil Requirements: Well-drained sandy loam soil
  • Watering Needs: Regular, every 3-4 days
  • Pesticide Use: Use fungicides to prevent blight and insecticides for aphids
  • Yield Per Acre: 20 tons
  • Market Price: ₹30 per kg
  • Nutritional Information: Rich in vitamins C and K, and a good source of folate and potassium
